About Us

Goal

The purpose of this website is to provide easy access to sudoku puzzles that people can solve to entertain and challenge themselves. You can play casually on the home page using our dynamically created suduko puzzles or try for your best time and get your name on the leaderboard with the daily puzzle. Broaden your skills by changing the difficulty or switching up the version of sudoku.

Creators

This website was created by a group of four students (Andrew DeChristopher, Enea Milo, Scott Austin, and James Lavigne) at Wentworth Institute of Technology.
